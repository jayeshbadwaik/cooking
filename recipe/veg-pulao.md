# Veg Pulao

## Ingredients

- Rice (2 cup)
- Pre-cut Frozen Veggie Mix (400gm)
- Green Cardamom (2)
- Cloves (2)
- Bay Leaf (1)
- Cinnamon Stick (1)


## Preparation

1. Wash the rice.
2. Mix the rice and veggies in instant pot.
3. Add the green cardamom, cloves, bay leaf, and cinnamon stick.
4. Add water and salt.
5. Cook on rice mode.
